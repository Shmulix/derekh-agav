Design a Hebrew RTL (right-to-left) car rental information website homepage.

Site name: דרך אגב (double meaning: "by the way" in Hebrew + "דרך" = road)
Tagline: "כי מי שיודע — לא מחכה לדלפק"

Brand personality: The expert friend. Someone who worked in car rental for 10+ years and is now telling you everything the rental companies don't want you to know. Direct, confident, slightly blunt — but professional and trustworthy. Think: premium editorial publication written by someone with real personality. NOT a government portal, NOT a corporate FAQ site.

The design must feel: authoritative but human. Structured but not stiff. High-trust with character.

---

## BRAND & COLORS

- Primary: #1a2f5e (deep navy blue)
- Accent: #c9a227 (warm gold)
- Background: #ffffff (main) / #f8f9fc (alternate sections)
- Text: #1a1a2e (near black)
- Font: Heebo (Hebrew-optimized Google Font) — clean, modern, highly readable
- Direction: RTL. All text right-aligned. Layout flows right to left.

---

## OVERALL STYLE

Ultra-professional. Think: government advisory site meets premium travel publication.
- Clean whitespace
- Strong typographic hierarchy (large bold H1, clear H2, readable body)
- Trust-first: credibility signals, author bio, expert badges
- Minimal use of color — navy and gold as accents only
- No rounded pill buttons (prefer solid rectangular or slightly rounded)
- Subtle hover states only

## ⚠️ IMPORTANT — THIS IS AN INFORMATION SITE, NOT A BOOKING SITE

This website does NOT allow users to search for cars, compare prices, or make reservations.
It is a Hebrew-language editorial guide written by an industry expert.

**Never include:**
- Date pickers (pickup date / return date)
- Location or airport search fields
- "Find a car" or "Search vehicles" forms
- Booking widgets or reservation engines
- Any multi-field search form in the hero or anywhere else

The ONLY link to car rental prices is a simple CTA button ("השווה מחירים →") that redirects to an affiliate partner. That's it.

---

## WHAT TO AVOID

- No gradients (except subtle overlay on hero)
- No rounded pill buttons (prefer 4-8px border-radius max)
- No stock photo clipart style images
- No neon colors
- No heavy drop shadows
- No centered RTL text (right-align is correct for Hebrew)
- No left-to-right layout (everything flows RTL)
